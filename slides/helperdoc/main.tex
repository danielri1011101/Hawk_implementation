\section{Field Extensions}
Consider the extension
\(
	K/\mathbb{Q}
\)
where
\[
	K= \mathbb{Q}[X]/
		\langle
		  X^4 + 1
		  \rangle.
\]

If we let $\alpha$ denote $\bar{X}$, then
\[
	\{
		1, \alpha, \alpha^2, \alpha^3
	\}
\]
constitutes a $\mathbb{Q}-$basis for $K$.

\section{Norms over Number Fields}
The \emph{inner product} over $K_n$
\[
	\langle, \rangle : K_n \times K_n \to \mathbb{Q}
\]
is defined as
\(
	\langle f, g \rangle= \frac{1}{n}\mathrm{Tr}(f^*g).
\)
